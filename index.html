Project Highlights

Selected recent achievements demonstrating our expertise and capability

2000MW Solar Project - Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Performed comprehensive civil structural engineering reviews as part of the Owner's Engineering team for this massive solar installation. The project included two Substations with 115kV double circuit overhead transmission lines and gantries. Scope included site studies, civil infrastructure studies, substation design of structures and foundations, OHTL structures.

Wind Turbine Foundation Design Evaluations - India, Vietnam, Thailand projects

Completed multiple wind turbine generator foundation design evaluations for projects based in India, Vietnam, Thailand and other international locations for a major certification body. These projects involved comprehensive structural and geotechnical analysis ensuring design compliance with international standards.

Wind Farms Technical Due Diligence - India

Conducted detailed technical due diligence evaluations of two operational wind farms in India for a major consultancy. The scope included comprehensive review of civil infrastructure, foundation design, structural integrity, and risk assessment with mitigation recommendations.

Ground mounted solar project technical review - Philippines

Conducted detailed structural review of civil engineering structures of the solar project in Philippines location and reviewed site studies such as Hydrgology, Topography, geotechnical studes and corrosion studies.

Wind Turbine Steel towers Manufacturing Evaluations - India

Performed Manufacturing evaluation (site inspections) of Tubular Steel tower plants in India according to IEC 61400-22:2010-5 as part of a Type Certification project for a major certification body. Manufacturing inspection included review of Quality Management system and manufacturing process review and inspection at site and delivery of reports.
